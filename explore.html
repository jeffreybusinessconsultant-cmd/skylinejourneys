Popular Destinations

Rajasthan - Land of Kings

Explore magnificent forts, palaces, and vibrant culture. Visit Jaipur's Pink City, Udaipur's romantic lakes, Jodhpur's Blue City, and Jaisalmer's golden desert. Experience royal heritage, camel safaris, and traditional Rajasthani hospitality.

Best Time: October to March | Duration: 7-10 days

Kerala - God's Own Country

Cruise through tranquil backwaters, relax on pristine beaches, and rejuvenate with Ayurvedic treatments. Explore tea plantations in Munnar, wildlife in Thekkady, and cultural performances in Kochi. Experience nature at its finest.

Best Time: September to March | Duration: 5-7 days

Himachal - Mountain Paradise

Adventure awaits in Manali, Shimla, and Dharamshala. Trek through pine forests, ski on snowy slopes, visit ancient temples, and experience Tibetan culture. Perfect for nature lovers, adventure seekers, and peace seekers alike.

Best Time: March to June, December to February | Duration: 5-8 days

Goa - Beach Capital

Sun, sand, and sea await at India's favorite beach destination. Enjoy water sports, vibrant nightlife, Portuguese architecture, and fresh seafood. From peaceful South Goa beaches to lively North Goa parties, there's something for everyone.

Best Time: November to February | Duration: 4-6 days

Andaman - Tropical Paradise

Discover pristine beaches, coral reefs, and marine life. Snorkel at Elephant Beach, relax at Radhanagar Beach, explore Cellular Jail's history, and witness bioluminescent beaches. A perfect tropical escape with crystal-clear waters.

Best Time: October to May | Duration: 6-8 days

Ladakh - Land of High Passes

Experience the world's highest motorable roads and stunning landscapes. Visit ancient monasteries, camp beside Pangong Lake, cross Khardung La Pass, and explore Nubra Valley. An adventure of a lifetime in the Himalayas.

Best Time: May to September | Duration: 8-10 days

Travel Tips & Insights

📋

Before You Go

  • Check visa requirements and validity
  • Get travel insurance coverage
  • Research local customs and etiquette
  • Pack according to weather and activities
  • Keep digital copies of important documents
💰

Budget Planning

  • Book flights 2-3 months in advance
  • Travel during off-peak seasons for deals
  • Use local transportation when possible
  • Try local street food for authentic experience
  • Look for package deals and group discounts
🎒

Packing Essentials

  • Comfortable walking shoes
  • Universal power adapter
  • First aid kit and medications
  • Reusable water bottle
  • Camera and extra batteries
