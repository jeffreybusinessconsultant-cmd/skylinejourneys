Who We Are

Our Story

Founded in 2014 in Guntur, Andhra Pradesh, SKY LINE JOURNEYS began with a simple vision: to make extraordinary travel experiences accessible to everyone. What started as a small team of three passionate travelers has grown into a trusted travel company serving over 15,000 satisfied customers across India.

Our founder, Rajesh Varma, a seasoned mountaineer and travel enthusiast, recognized that many Indians dreamed of exploring their own country but lacked the guidance and resources. With this insight, he assembled a team of experienced travel professionals, local guides, and hospitality experts to create authentic, affordable, and safe travel experiences.

Today, we operate tours across 28 Indian states and 4 union territories, partnering with over 200 local hotels, 50+ experienced guides, and maintaining a fleet of 25 well-maintained vehicles. Our commitment to sustainable tourism and supporting local communities sets us apart in the industry.
